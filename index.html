Who I Am

My name is Vlad Kolmorgan, and I am a 23-year-old coder passionate about coding and video game design. My love for game development and coding has been with me since my childhood, it has always been a dream of mine. However, dreams remain dreams unless they are pursued. To turn my dream into reality I made it a goal. After years of education I have reached the starting point in the next step of my journey, and I look forward to the many years of coding ahead.

Purpose Of Website

To further my goals of programming I created this website. This website is a showcase that shows examples of my coding practices, shares the things I have learned, and serves as a way to let others learn more about me. Through my experience with languages such as HTML, C++, and Python along with tools such as GitHub, Jira, and databases I hope to demonstrate that I am both a passionate and capable coder.
