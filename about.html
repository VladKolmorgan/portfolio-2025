About Myself

My name is Vlad Kolmorgan, and I am an aspiring Game Developer. I hold an Associate of Applied Science (AAS) in Software Development, and while I have been taught a great deal from earning that degree, learning never stops especially for programmers. I’ve always had a passion for gaming, and this has made me have an increasing desire to join the gaming industry. Now that I have completed my AAS I will begin my next steps in life. Soon, I won’t just be playing the games I love, I'll also be creating them.

My Goals

I have set some goals that I am currently working towards. My primary goal is to become a game developer. This has always been a dream of mine, and I would like to work towards becoming one. To this end, I have an additional goal of making a game on my own. This project will serve as a showcase of my skills allowing others to view my work as a developer. Currently, this game is still underdevelopment and will be added to the website after it is done. In order to achieve my previous goals I will need a way to show off my skills and work. To this end, I have designed a portfolio website where potential employers and other visitors can see my work and learn more about me.
